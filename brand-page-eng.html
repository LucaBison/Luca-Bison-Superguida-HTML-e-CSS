Our wines

The sandy and limestone soils and the perfect combination of ventilation and temperature leap between night and day, guarantee the ideal terroir for the growth of the Sangiovese grapes, giving to it a good acidity and an elegant tannin and a naturally vocation to the aging. Over the Sangiovese grapes, which is the historic autochthone vineyard of the Chianti zone, are cultivated other kind of international grapes, like Cabernet franc, Merlot and Syrah.
